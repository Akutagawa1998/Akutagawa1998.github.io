ABOUT ME:

My name is ZHAO, Sihang, you can also call me "Nagi"(凪), which is my Japanese and English name.
I was diagnosed with ADHD in 2022. So please excuse my (maybe too) wild imagination and frequent moments of sudden, deep contemplation :) I am an typical ENTP who scored 142 on MENSA IQ Challenge.
Basketball is my another life. I played for Imperial College London Men's Basketball Team as SG during 2021~2022.
You can find my photography work in my Instagram. The theme of my photos is the journey in search of peace and harmony, alongside the loneliness and melancholy felt along the way.

RESEARCH INTERESTS:

My primary research interest lies in the AI-based interactive-accessible design for neurodiversity (such as for dyslexia, ADHD). I am also interested in security, bias and fairness issues in generative models, especially large language models.

Currently, I'm looking for doctoral opportunities in human-computer interaction, AI Safety and applied machine learning. I'm applying for PhD in the U.S.A. in 2025 Fall.
