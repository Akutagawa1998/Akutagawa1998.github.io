ABOUT ME:

My name is ZHAO, Sihang, you can also call me "Nagi"(凪), which is my Japanese and English name.

RESEARCH INTERESTS:

My primary research interest lies in the development of neurodiversity-friendly interactive software (such as for dyslexia, ADHD), as well as accessible design based on machine learning. I am also interested in security and fairness issues in generative models, especially large language models.

Currently, I'm looking for doctoral opportunities in human-computer interaction, AI Safety and applied machine learning. I'm open considering universities and research institutions in North America, Europe, Singapore, Hong Kong, and Japan.
